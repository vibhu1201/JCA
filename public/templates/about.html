Background

Our commitment is to patients first. We are committed to increasing knowledge through continuous education. Know that you have alternatives and that we will be there to help you choose whatever is best for your situation and lifestyle.

With his extensive experience, John has worked with thousands of clients, building up a clientele that would be greatly appreciated by any stylist. The years of service he has provided his clients has developed into some very strong relationships.

Many close family and friends had experienced various types of cancer, but John never knew how to help until he discovered the alternative hair business. His passion for the wig business stems from the solace women find when they realize there is a solution for their hair loss due to cancer treatment, trichotillomania or alopecia.

His innate ability to provide love, support and humor throughout this process is why many of his clients have commented that he has made a difficult experience easier than they ever imagined.

F.A.Q's

What kind of wig should I get?

Although there are only 2 options, human or synthetic hair, there are numerous choices.

What kind of choices do I have?

Choices range from synthetic hair wigs (short length, machine-made) to European human hair wigs (long length, hand-tied, lace-front), from light blond to dark black. The colors come highlighted, low lighted or root color. Hair texture ranges from Anglo European to ethnic.

Are wigs usually covered by insurance?

You will need to contact your insurance carrier to verify coverage under your policy. We are confident we can work within any budget.

How easy will my new hair be to care for?

One of the things we will talk about during our consultation is your current styling habits. We then make a hairstyle choice that best suits your needs.

What is your Refund Policy?

We begin with a complimentary 2 to 3 hour initial consultation that provides our customers with a clear understanding of J. Crager Alternatives products and services. Part of this consultation covers our refund policy.

Non-Customized Wigs: A full refund is offered on wigs that have not been cut, colored, resized or altered in any way.

Customized or Altered Wigs:We have a no return/no refund policy on custom wig orders, or any wig that has been cut, colored, resized or altered in any fashion. j.crager Alternatives works with each customer to ensure the wig is cut, sized, and colored to their satisfaction. Part of the styling and sizing process includes in-depth product care and maintenance education. Without proper care and maintenance, the quality of any product will be short lived.
