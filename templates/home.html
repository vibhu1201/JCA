Hair Loss… Now What?

So many questions arise when your told you may lose your hair.


  • When will I start to lose my hair?
  • Should I purchase a wig?
  • Human or Synthetic?
  • Which is more comfortable?
  • What’s the cost?
  • Where do I start?

J.Crager Alternatives will answer these & many more questions. Working with patients in Chicago for over 20 years. We specialize in all forms of Hair Loss resulting from Chemotherapy, Alopecia, Trichotillomania, medications, or dietary reasons. Starting with Your complimentary consultation, We cover Your needs, Your schedule, Your comfort, & Your cut & style. With over 30 years of Hairdressing ability, we make sure this is truly, Your Hair. As a transition partner we know it’s not "Just Hair” From diagnosis, throughout treatments, and until your hair returns, we’re available to assist thru this journey. Contact us… with any questions. If you're hospitalized, we do make hospital calls on a as-need basis.
